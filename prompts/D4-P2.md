# D4-P2: Draft a Message

## Prompt
```
Role: You are a professional communication assistant.
Audience: A university student writing to their professor.

Input: Draft an email to Professor Zhang requesting an extension on the AI+X Elite 20 assignment deadline by 2 days due to network issues preventing access to Claude for the smoke-test.

Output Shape:
- Professional email format (subject, greeting, body, sign-off)
- Polite and concise (under 150 words)
- Include a specific new deadline

Acceptance: The email should be respectful, explain the situation clearly, and propose a reasonable solution.
```

## Response
**Subject:** Request for Extension on AI+X Elite 20 Assignment - Liu Tingting

Dear Professor Zhang,

I hope this email finds you well. I am writing to respectfully request a 2-day extension on the current AI+X Elite 20 assignment.

Due to persistent network connectivity issues, I have been unable to access Claude desktop to complete the required environment smoke-test. I have already set up my workspace folder and am ready to proceed once the connection stabilizes.

Would it be possible to submit the assignment by [new deadline] instead? I understand the importance of keeping up with the course schedule and will ensure this does not become a recurring issue.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Best regards,
Liu Tingting
AI+X Elite 20 Student
